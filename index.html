James Tobin

Work grounded in communication, judgement, and the ability to read tone and intent in conversation.

Armed with an early passion for technology, James began his career in recruitment, developing expertise in talent advisory and commercial strategy and quickly establishing his own firm.

He moved into the mobile and telecommunications sector during the growth of premium-rate entertainment services — an early precursor to social entertainment — contributing to the development and commercialisation of interactive voice response and speech recognition products.

Alongside this, James explored the internet economy — trading domain names and building a subscription content platform around flat-rate billing, undercutting the pricing competitors relied on.

James then returned to recruitment, bringing experience from technology and creative industries into a more commercially informed approach.

Today, James incubates early-stage ventures — including, at one point, an estate agency built just to sell his own house — alongside creative and experimental projects, all whilst continuing to run camalyn.
